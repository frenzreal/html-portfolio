Finding calm in the
beautiful puzzle of life

A Piece of My Sanity is a brand rooted in mindfulness, self-care, and the quiet joy of slowing down. Like a puzzle coming together, it celebrates small moments of peace — the golden hour, still water, a solitary tree — that restore us when life feels fragmented.

02 — Typography

Script · Brand Accent
Dancing Script
Bold 700 · Brand wordmark "Sanity"
Used for: Logo wordmark, callouts, special emphasis · Never use for body text or long passages
Body · Utility
Lato
Lato provides clean, highly legible text for longer reads — articles, captions, product descriptions, and UI elements. Its warmth pairs beautifully with the more expressive display faces.
Weights: Light 300, Regular 400, Bold 700 · Use 300/400 for body, 700 for labels & UI elements

05 — Voice
Tone of Voice

✓ We sound like this

  • Warm, encouraging, and real
  • Conversational — as if talking to a friend
  • Thoughtful and reflective
  • Gently empowering — never preachy
  • Specific and grounded, not vague
  • Celebrates small wins and imperfection

✗ We don't sound like this

  • Clinical or overly technical
  • Hustle-culture or productivity-obsessed
  • Preachy or self-righteous
  • Toxic positivity ("Good vibes only!")
  • Corporate jargon
  • Negative or shame-inducing
Example Phrasings
✓ Say this
"Every small step toward calm matters."
✗ Not this
"Optimize your daily wellness routine."
